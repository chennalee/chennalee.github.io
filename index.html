about

Chenna (ChaeEun) Lee is a composer and sound artist from South Korea, based in San Francisco. With a strong passion for exploring the impact that sound has on us, she creates music and sound that help connect to deeper emotions and experiences.

Her collaborations include scoring the online RPG game Three Kingdoms of Romance, published by Weaver Interactive game (Korea), and composing arrival music for the conference Sleep & Dreams: Music, Neuroscience & Stories of Slumber, presented by San Francisco Conservatory of Music, UC San Francisco Department of Neurology, and The Nocturnists. Chennaâ€™s expertise in orchestration earned her recognition as a winner of the 2024 KDFC Sound Logo Competition, where she arranged eight logo songs for the Classical KDFC Radio Station.

Beyond her composition achievements, Chenna contributed to research on audiovisual cross-modal perception, resulting in publications and poster presentations at renowned international journals and conferences.

Chenna holds a Master of Music degree in Technology and Applied Composition from San Francisco Conservatory of Music, where she studied under the supervision of Daria Novo, and a Bachelor of Music in Composition from Seoul National University.
